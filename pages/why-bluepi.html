Why join BluePi

Exciting projects

We are doing transformative projects for our customers, helping them benefit from the latest in data and cloud technologies. Some picture of the latest tech to highlight AWS, K8S, Databricks, Tensorflow, PyTorch, React, Snowflake.

No Hierarchy

You hate red tape, so do we. We are a group of self-driven people working together to achieve the impossible for the customer. Empowerment is a value we live by, and we expect entrepreneurial spirit in every one of us.

Career Progression

You own your career progression at BluePi. However, we guide you through the process with our mentorship programme and clear articulation and tracking of short and long term objectives. Our projects will challenge you out of your comfort zone and set you up for the next role.

Education

We also have an extensive training curriculum to hone your skills. Your mentor works with you to identify the skills needed and you will have access to the best in class and cutting edge instructor-led and on-demand trainings.
